ABOUT US

Why Life Planner?

Life Planner, from its modest start-up in 2012, has grown into a reputed and well-respected educational facilitator among study abroad consultants in Kottayam, Kerala. Accredited with an ISO certification, our trustworthy service offers detailed counseling for each student throughout admission and visa processing, catering to their individual talent, interests, and study skills.

Our team provides admission guidance for Medical, Engineering, Paramedical, Nursing, IT, Technical, Management, and multiple other academic streams in reputed universities and institutions worldwide. Options include undergraduate and postgraduate programs, certificate programs, and diploma courses in almost all countries. Our own Academy ensures you achieve excellent IELTS/German language scores through expert training.

We assist international students in finding jobs post-studies through our Alumnus and Immigration Consultant Office. Life Planner continues to thrive as one of the leading overseas education consultants in Kottayam, providing support even after students reach abroad.

Our Services

  • Expert advice to match your interests with the best programs.
  • Personalized care and attention throughout all stages of processing.
  • Frequent spot admissions/seminar sessions at our offices.
  • Scholarship/fee discounts from selected institutions based on criteria.
  • Free airport pickup and accommodation assistance.
  • Free visa guidance and education loan assistance.
  • Forex services and competitive air ticketing rates.
  • Access to references from students who studied abroad through us.
  • 100% transparent services.

Our Commitment

Life Planner Studies and Opportunities Pvt Ltd, headquartered in Kottayam with a corporate office in Cochin, is dedicated to ensuring our students' smooth transition abroad. We coordinate with university/college officials and our former students, who are well-settled, to provide airport pickups and stay arrangements. This collective effort is made possible by the excellent service we have consistently delivered over the years.

Join Life Planner, and let us help you achieve your academic and professional aspirations with unparalleled guidance and support.
