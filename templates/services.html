OUR SERVICE

Seminars & Webinars

Informative seminars and webinars provide detailed guidance on studying abroad. Cover essential aspects, including university programs, admission requirements, and more. Interactive online sessions are available for those unable to attend in person. Live Q&A sessions address specific queries from students. Post-seminar, each student receives: One-on-one counselling to discuss academic and career aspirations. Support from an admission executive throughout the application process.

Academic & Career Counselling

Comprehensive evaluation of the student’s: Academic background and work experience. Language proficiency, particularly in German. Assessment ensures a match with the right academic program and career opportunities. Evaluate the student’s financial readiness for studying and living abroad.

Assessment on Profile

Personalised counselling based on the profile assessment. Identify the fields that align best with the student’s interests and goals. Recommend skill development programs, such as: Improving German language proficiency. Preparing for exams like IELTS. Provide insights into the job market and conduct: Mock interviews to boost confidence. Industry-specific guidance for future career success.

Admission & Enrollment

Guide students through the admission and enrollment process. Assist in preparing: Application materials, including resumes, personal statements, and cover letters. Materials that meet country- and university-specific requirements. Provide details on: Fee structure Duration of the study program Curriculum and Coursework Focus of the Program Support students with language proficiency requirements, focusing on German and English.

Financial Aid Guidance

Help students understand the financial requirements for studying abroad. Provide guidance on visa-related financial documentation, such as: Bank statements. Financial sponsorship letters. Explore available financial aid options, including: Scholarships. Student loans.

Visa

Full guidance on meeting visa requirements and selecting the correct visa type. Assist in gathering and preparing the required visa documentation. Ensure all documents meet embassy standards. Prepare students for their visa interview to maximise success.

Pre-Departure

Provide a comprehensive pre-departure session covering: Travel details, including flight bookings. Accommodation arrangements at the study destination. Country-specific guidelines for a smooth transition. Ensure students feel prepared for their move abroad and understand next steps.

Post-Landing

Support students upon arrival with: Immigration procedures. Guidance on transport options. Assist students in settling into their new environment. Provide ongoing support to help students adjust confidently to their new life abroad.
